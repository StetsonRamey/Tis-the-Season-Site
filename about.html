Who we are and what we're all about

Local Kansas City business owners who have a great time hanging holiday lights for great people.

At Tis the Season, our whole goal is to have a good time installing our holiday lighitng and make customers holidays a little brighter. We respect the home we're working on and each of our installs are clean, correct, and look great. If there's an issue, politely communicate with us, and then we can work together to make it right. We hope our work improves your holiday season and that you get the maximum enjoyment from your holiday lighting.

Our Values

The core values of Tis the Season

Communication

We want to communicate clearly about what you want, price, and schedule. We offer recommendations and learn what you want on our initial call. We provide a guaranteed estimated price over the phone so there are no surprises. And we communicate early and often via text message about our schedule.

Fun

We get genuinely excited about the holiday lighting season. We have a high rate of return customers and it's great getting to talk to everyone again and get our schedule built. There is also a high level of satisfaction our team gets from seeing peoples homes lit up.

Exceptional

Our aim is deliver both exceptional product and exceptional service. From our initial phone call to when we remove the lights in January we want our customers to love our service. We also strive to buy and use the highest quality products, so your home stands out from the crowd.

Easy to work with

We make it easy to get ahold of us via phone / email / or text message. And if we can't respond immediately, we will within a maximum of 24 hours. If there's something you want to know ask us and if we don't know, we'll figure it out.
