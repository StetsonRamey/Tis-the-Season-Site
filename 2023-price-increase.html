Notice: 2023 Price Increase

Starting next year (holiday season 2023) we will increase our installation price by $0.25/ft. Please read below for more details.

The Important Details

  • Amount of Increase: We are increasing the price of re-installation from $2.00/ft to $2.25/ft.
  • Last Price Increase: We haven't increased our price since 2016 (when we started the company) and do not plan on raising our prices again soon.
  • What you need to do: Nothing! Our processes will remain the same. We'll send out signup links in Sept. 2023 and your invoice will reflect our new pricing.
  • If you don't want to work with us due to the price increase: Please send us a text to let us know and we'll leave your lights at your home in January when we take them down.

More Details:

    Why the increase?
  • We are increasing our costs to keep pace with our rising business costs to provide our service. Most notably our storage, software, and labor have increased since 2016.
    • Will this become an annual increase?
  • We do not plan on increasing our prices annually. We've planned this increase so that we can provide the same great level of service for years to come without having to raise our prices every year.
    • Is the re-install price the only thing that changes?
  • Yes. Your lights and your linear footage (which can be found on your invoice) stays exactly the same. The service we provide will not change. We are only changing the price of re-installation from $2.00/ft to $2.25/ft.
