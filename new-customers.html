Important Information for New Customers

We're very excited to work with you this year and hope we work together for many years to come. Here is some info so you know what to expect from us.

What we need from you on the day we install

Please leave extension cord and timer (optional) on front porch

  • If you do not have soffit outlets, a 25' extension cord to power up your lights

  • If you do have soffit outlets, please turn soffit outlet switch on so we can power your lights and check all the bulbs.

  • A timer if you'd like us to install one inline. Home Depot, Lowes, or this one from Amazon are typical.

    NOTE: we will not install dial timers on soffit outlets. If you have soffit outlets and want a timer, it must be a photocell timer.

Payment

We will not install your lights unless your invoice is paid or you've communicated with us that you're leaving payment or paying in person.

If paying electronically, payment is due the evening before your scheduled install

  • We accept the following forms of payment:

    1. Online via any major debit / credit card through the payment link we text you

    2. Cash / Check in person

  • Failure to pay or communicate about payment will delay your installation.

How we'll communicate with you

  • You'll recieve your invoice link via text message

  • We'll send a reminder text message the day before your scheduled install. If we are delayed by weather we'll communicate the delay via text message.

  • We will never text you other than for new season signups and schedule updates, and you may opt out at any time. If you opt out of text updates, you will not receive the most up to date information and it will be your responsibility to contact us directly to get updates.

  • We'll send texts with our takedown schedule after Christmas.
