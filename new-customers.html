Important Information for New Customers

We're very excited to work with you this year and hope we work together for many years to come. Here is some info so you know what to expect from us.

What we need from you on the day we install

Please leave extension cord and timer (optional) on front porch

  • If you do not have soffit plugs, a 25' extension cord to power up your lights

  • If you do have soffit plugs, please turn soffit plug switch on so we can power your lights and check all the bulbs.

  • A timer if you'd like us to install one inline. Home Depot, Lowes, or this one from Amazon are typical.

Payment

We will not install your lights unless your invoice is paid or you've communicated with us that you're leaving payment or paying in person.

If paying electronically, payment is due the evening before your scheduled install

  • You may pay electronically through the invoice we email you. We accept:

    1. Preferred: ACH (electronic check). Free for us and just like a check

    2. Credit Card: We pay a 3% processing fee. Costs us money, but convenient for you.

    3. Cash / Physical Check in person

  • Failure to pay or communicate about payment will delay your installation.

How we'll communicate with you

  • You'll recieve your invoice via email. It comes from
    'Tis the Season for Lights - quickbooks@notification.intuit.com
    Check your spam folder or call us if you don't see it.

  • We'll text & email you with your scheduled date and the details we have for your install. The day before we install your group, we'll text you to let you know. Please keep in mind weather delays can change our schedule and we will update you if they do.

  • We will never text you other than for new season signups and schedule updates, and you may opt out at any time. If you opt out of text updates, you will not receive the most up to date information and will need to contact us directly to get updates.
