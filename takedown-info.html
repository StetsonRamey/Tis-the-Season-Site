Important Information for Lighting Takedowns

Please read the following information and as always if you have question give us a call or shoot us a text!

Takedown Schedule

We run our takedowns by group and area. We cannot accomodate specific date requests. We strive to get everyone's lights down and stored in as timely a manner as weather and daylight will allow.

  • You'll receive a text & email with a date range we will be taking down your lights in

  • If you wish to store your own lights, you are responsible for texting or calling and communicating that with us. Otherwise we assume that we are storing your lights and we will take them with us when we take them down.

  • We only store the lights we hang on your house. Wreaths we will leave on the front porch for you to store until next season.

Storing Your Own Lights

  • If you self-stored last year, we left your rubbermaid tote on your porch after we installed. Please leave the tote on your front porch for us to put your lights in.

    Failure to leave your tote on the front porch will result in a charge on next years bill for a new tote.

  • If this is the first year you worked with us and you want to store your lights, you must text or call us and let us know BEFORE we have taken your lights down.

    If you request to store your own lights after we have taken them down, we may charge a return trip fee of at least $75 to return to your home and drop off your lights.

The Yearly Goal

  • Every year we aim to have everyone's lights down before February. That is our goal this year as well.

  • Weather can and seems to always delay our schedule. We cannot remove your lights if there is ice in your gutters and our clips/wire are frozen to them. We risk damage to your lights and your home if we encounter these conditions and we will wait until the ice melts.

  • As always, we are a text or phone call away if you want an update on what's going on. We will be working every day that we can until all the lights are down, and will answer texts by the end of the day and calls within 24 hours.
